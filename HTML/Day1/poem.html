Love For Nature

            In forests deep and mountains high,
            where birds sing sweetly in the sky.
            I find a peace that fills my soul,
            In nature's arms, I feel whole.

            The gentle breeze, the rustling trees,
            The buzzing of the honeybees.
            Each leaf that falls, each flower's bloom,
            Nature's beauty, banishes gloom.

            The sprakling streams,the rivers wide,
            Reflect the beauty deep inside.
            The earth beneath my weary feet.
            Is where my heart finds its retreat.
            
poet : Sampa Nayak
